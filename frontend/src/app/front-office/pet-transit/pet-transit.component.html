Pet Transit

Plan transport and handoff flows for pets

This module is ready for your teammate to build booking, route planning, driver assignment, and status tracking on top of a stable front-office entry point.

Suggested Scope

Route Planning

Transfer requests between clinics, shelters, homes, foster families, and boarding locations.

Status Tracking

Pickup scheduled, en route, arrived, delayed, and completed status milestones.

Pet Readiness

Medication notes, transport crate instructions, emergency contacts, and special handling.

Coordination

Messages between owners, providers, shelters, and transport operators before handoff.

Access Model

Visitors

Can browse transit capabilities and understand the flow before signing in.

Signed-in users

Will later request transport, attach pets, and follow delivery progress.
